Golden Ears

 
 

    Welcome to our Cattery page, we are pleased to see that you are interested in the Scottish fold breed. Scottish Fold is a remarkable breed, because they are very independent and clean. Scottish Fold cats are the only cats that have folded ears, which makes them the cutest cats in the world. Scottish Fold cats have a very interesting personality point, they can sit in “the Buddha sit” position. This is a position where they are sitting on the floor with their belly up and it looks as if they are sitting on a couch.


    Overall, Scottish Folds make a great addition to any family, they are very friendly with the children and other family members.
